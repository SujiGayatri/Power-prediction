About Our Project

Power Estimation System is an AI-driven platform that predicts electricity demand using weather parameters, load conditions and temporal patterns to help improve smart grid efficiency.

⚡ What We Built

Our system uses a hybrid **CNN-BiLSTM deep learning model** to forecast electricity demand. The model analyzes environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, rainfall, time and grid load to predict energy consumption. This helps energy providers: • Predict demand spikes • Improve grid stability • Reduce operational cost • Monitor environmental impact The system also calculates **CO₂ emissions and energy cost predictions** for better sustainability planning.

🎯 Our Mission

To develop intelligent energy forecasting systems that improve smart grid management, optimize electricity consumption and support sustainable energy infrastructure.

🚀 Our Vision

To leverage artificial intelligence and deep learning technologies to create next-generation energy analytics platforms that support global energy sustainability.
